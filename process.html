How I Design Learning

My framework for creating learning experiences that stick and scale.

Discovery & Needs Analysis

I start by understanding the audience, business goals and existing gaps so the solution is grounded in reality.

Design & Development

I map objectives to outcomes and build engaging content—mixing modalities like video, hands-on practice and AI simulations.

Delivery & Adoption

Rolling out training isn’t the end—embedding it into workflows and ensuring adoption matters just as much.

Evaluation & Iteration

I collect feedback and performance data to measure impact and iterate the program to stay relevant.
