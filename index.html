About

Hi, I’m Kyle. I build learning that helps sales orgs do their jobs better — faster. My sweet spots are seller onboarding, scenario design, and AI‑assisted simulations for objection handling, discovery, and value articulation.

I started in higher ed teaching public speaking, then shifted to corporate L&D. Since then, I’ve designed programs for teams at tech and fintech companies, top law firms, and global partner ecosystems. I care about clarity, business impact, and making practice safe, scalable, and sticky.

Focus: onboarding & accreditation • simulations & scenarios • certification prep • LMS implementation (Cornerstone)

  • Video: Camtasia, Descript

  • Data: xAPI basics, dashboards
  • AI: prompt systems for branching, role‑play bots
