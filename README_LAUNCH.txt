1) Open index.html locally to verify.

3) Replace FormSubmit endpoint in index.html: find YOUREMAIL@example.com -> kpcolgla@gmail.com (or your FormSubmit endpoint).

5) Test live: theme toggle, smooth scrolling, timeline expand, résumé click, contact form.
